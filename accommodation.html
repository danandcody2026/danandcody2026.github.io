FERRY WARNING

Please be aware that the Fowey–Polruan and Fowey–Bodinnick ferries do not run through the night. If you're staying across the water and relying on the ferry, plan for a significantly longer drive home via the road route, and be sure to check the ferry timetables closer to the date!

Staying On-Site at Tremeer Farm

Welcome to the Farm!

We are absolutely over the moon that so many of you are joining us at Tremeer Farm for the whole weekend. Whether you are tucked up in the cottage, cosy in a yurt, pitching a tent, or rolling in your van, we cannot wait to share the space with you.

Hopefully, by now, you already know exactly which rustic retreat is yours.

The Key Details

To make sure everyone has a smooth weekend, here are the essential logistics:

Arrival

From 4:00 PM on Friday, 12th June 2026

Departure

By 10:00 AM on Monday, 15th June

Friday Night Setup and Dinner

We are so grateful for everyone pitching in. As a huge thank you for your invaluable help setting up, decorating, and transforming the barn on Friday, we will be providing a proper dinner for the entire crew that evening.

Life on the Farm

Wifi

Yes, there is Wifi across the site if you need to connect.

For Yurt Guests

Each yurt comes complete with its own private hammock and firepit, which is ideal for a morning coffee or a late-night chat.

Communal Spaces

There are shared kitchen spaces available for everyone staying on site.

A Note on Food and Drink

While we are providing Friday's dinner and will have basic breakfast provisions available on Saturday morning, you are very welcome to bring your own specific food bits to enjoy throughout the rest of the weekend.

The Golden Fridge Rule

Please keep fridge-stored food to an absolute minimum. The shared fridges will be almost entirely prioritised for keeping the beer, cider, and pasties cold.

Payment

To simplify things, we are handling the collection of accommodation fees directly, which we will then pay to the venue on your behalf.

FERRY WARNING

Please be aware that the Fowey–Polruan and Fowey–Bodinnick ferries do not run through the night. If you're staying across the estuary, plan for a significantly longer drive home in the evening and be sure to check the ferry timetables closer to the date!

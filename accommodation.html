You're staying on-site at Tremeer Farm for the weekend, a wildflower meadow site with yurts, a cottage, and tent pitches. Have a nosey at their Airbnb listing for the full picture.

It's worth packing sturdy shoes or wellies. The site has paths mown through a wildflower meadow, which can get a bit muddy if it's been raining.

Parking

There is parking on-site but space is limited, so we'd really appreciate any car sharing you can manage. We're also looking into a shuttle from a nearby car park and will update this page with more details closer to the date.

Payment

FERRY WARNING

Please be aware that the Fowey–Polruan and Fowey–Bodinnick ferries do not run through the night. If you're staying across the estuary, plan for a significantly longer drive home in the evening and be sure to check the ferry timetables closer to the date!

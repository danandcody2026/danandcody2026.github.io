What should I wear?

Dress festively, but be prepared to dance. If wearing a suit & tie or power suit for the evening is your “festive,” go for it! If you’re more into a pants+sneakers+fun shirt, that works too. The same goes for dresses, jumpsuits, etc... Nothing is required, but lean colorful / fun / your “most complicated shirt.” At night it will get chillier, so bring a layer!

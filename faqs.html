Got questions? We've (hopefully) got answers. If not, give us a shout!

What should I wear?

We genuinely do not mind what you wear. Come in whatever you feel best in!

That said, lots of people have asked for some guidance so no one feels particularly over- or underdressed. The general vibe is a casual summer garden party with plenty of colour. For a rough baseline: Danny will likely wear a jacket and shirt (no tie), and Cody will be in a white dress. Suits and ties are entirely optional.

Practical notes:

  • Footwear: most of the outdoor space is grass, so factor that into your shoe choice.
  • Weather: it may get cooler in the evening or rain, so bringing layers is sensible. Don't worry too much about the cold, though; we'll be providing beer jackets anyway.
Can I bring a plus one?

We'd love to say yes to everyone, but unfortunately we can only accomodate guests who are specifically named on the invitations. Thanks for understanding, we can't wait to celebrate with you!

Is there parking at the venue?

Yes! There is free parking available on site.

What if I have dietary requirements?

Please make sure you detail any dietary requirements in your RSVP form. If you've forgotten to do this, please let us know! All food served at dinner will be vegetarian, and there will be veggie/vegan options for pasties too.

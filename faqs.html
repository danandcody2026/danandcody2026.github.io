WHAT SHOULD I WEAR?

We genuinely do not mind what you wear. Come in whatever you feel best in! That said, lots of people have asked for some guidance, so hopefully this will help:

The general vibe is a casual summer garden party with plenty of colour. For a rough baseline: Danny will be wearing a jacket and shirt (no tie), and Cody will be in a white dress. Suits and ties are entirely optional.

Practical notes:

  • Footwear: most of the outdoor space is grass, so factor that into your shoe choice.
  • Weather: it may get cooler in the evening and there is always a risk of rain, so bringing layers is sensible (although we will of course be providing beer jackets...)

CAN I BRING A PLUS ONE?

We'd love to say yes to everyone, but unfortunately we can only accommodate guests who are specifically named on the invitations. Thanks for understanding, we can't wait to celebrate with you!

WHAT AND WHERE IS TREMEER FARM?

Tremeer Farm is a countryside yurt glamping venue near Fowey in Cornwall, with open fields, and plenty of outdoor space for a relaxed weekend wedding.

Address: Tremeer Farm, Fowey, Cornwall, PL23 1NP.

Feeling nosey? Explore Tremeer Farm.

HOW DO I GET TO AND FROM TREMEER?

The nearest train station is Par (10 min taxi). Be warned, Ubers are few and far between in Cornwall. We recommend these local taxi companies instead:

  • K.P Cabs – 07857 636881
  • Yeo's Taxis – 01726 814095
  • Killigarth Taxi Service – 01503 272818
  • Looe Taxis – 01503 262405

We strongly advise booking earlier rather than later and sharing lifts where possible!

IS THERE PARKING AT THE VENUE?

While there will be some parking available on site, this is limited. Please try and arrive via taxi or share lifts where possible. If you would prefer to park at the venue, drop us a message so we have a rough idea of numbers. We're also looking into a shuttle from a nearby car park and will update with more details closer to the date.

WHAT IF I HAVE DIETARY REQUIREMENTS?

Please detail any dietary requirements or allergies in your RSVP. All food will be vegetarian, with vegan options available. Pasties will also be offered with meat options.

IS THERE A GIFT LIST?

We genuinely don't want gifts (no, seriously). If you really can't resist, we'd much rather you made a small contribution to the bar. Head to our drinks registry to pick something you'd love to see at the party.

WHAT'S THE PLAN?

If we actually knew the plan, we'd tell you... but for now, here's a rough idea of what to expect:

Location

We'll be celebrating at Tremeer Farm, just outside of Fowey, Cornwall. It's a gorgeous spot tucked away in the countryside, complete with farm animals, a wood-fired hothub, and even a sauna! It's also close to two of Cornwall's most picturesque beaches: Lantic Bay and Lansallos Cove.

Get directions

The Weekend

The main celebration will take place on the Saturday, but we'll be at Tremeer from Friday afternoon to Monday morning. Friday evening will be all hands on deck with setting up, and Sunday will likely involve a Cornish sea swim to cure the hangovers... You're all welcome to join us for as much or as little of that as you like!

Accommodation

The venue has some limited camping space on-site, and there are also plenty of other campsites and Airbnbs nearby. For those who don't fancy camping, we will share some accommodation suggestions soon.
